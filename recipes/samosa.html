Irresistible Samosa Recipe - Crispy Triangles of Savory Delight

Back to Home-Page

Introduction:

Step into the world of delectable Indian street food with our tantalizing Samosa recipe. These golden-brown, crispy, and flavorful triangular pastries are a beloved treat loved by millions around the globe. Filled with a savory mixture of spiced potatoes and peas, wrapped in a delicate yet crispy crust, Samosas are the ultimate indulgence for your taste buds. Whether you crave a satisfying snack or an appetizer to impress your guests, this timeless recipe is sure to be a hit!

Ingredients:

For the Dough:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil or ghee
  • 1/2 teaspoon carom seeds (ajwain)
  • A pinch of salt
  • Cold water, as needed

For the Filling:

  • 3 large potatoes, boiled and mashed
  • 1/2 cup green peas, boiled (fresh or frozen)
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2-3 green chilies, finely chopped (adjust to taste)
  • 1 teaspoon grated ginger
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder (adjust to spice preference)
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 2 tablespoons chopped cilantro (coriander) leaves
  • Salt, to taste

Instructions:

  1. Make the Dough: Mix flour, oil, carom seeds, salt, and water to form a smooth dough. Let it rest.
  2. Prepare the Filling: Saut√© onions, ginger, and chilies. Add mashed potatoes, peas, spices, and cilantro. Cook and cool.
  3. Assemble: Roll dough into ovals, cut in half, and form cones. Fill with the mixture and seal the edges.
  4. Fry: Deep fry the samosas until golden-brown and crispy.
  5. Serve: Enjoy hot with chutney or on their own as a delightful snack!

Our homemade Samosas are a true taste of Indian street food magic, sure to impress at any gathering or as a quick and delicious treat.
